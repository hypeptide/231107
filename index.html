COMPANY PROFILE
KS-V Peptide Biotechnology Co., Ltd.was founded in 2018 by three internationally renowned scientists from China University of Science and
Technology, Tsinghua University, and Nankai University. Our company has several cutting-edge core technologies and four
major platforms, including peptide synthesis platform, peptide drug discovery platform, structural biology platform, as well as pre-clinical testing platform. We can offer comprehensive support for integrated drug discovery and development services.
